Grease Trap Service

Keep Your Kitchen Compliant and Running

Grease trap management is a legal requirement and operational necessity for every commercial kitchen. JR-Tech provides scheduled cleaning, bio-treatment dosing and compliance documentation to keep your kitchen operating without interruption. Our bio-treatment solutions extend cleaning intervals and reduce disposal costs.

  • Scheduled grease trap cleaning programmes
  • Bio-treatment dosing systems
  • Compliance documentation for authority submissions
  • Emergency grease trap service available
Scheduled Cleaning Programmes
Regular cleaning on a schedule that matches your kitchen volume — monthly, quarterly, or custom.
Bio-Treatment Dosing
Enzymatic dosing systems reduce grease build-up between cleans, extending intervals and lowering disposal costs.
Compliance Documentation
