About JR-Tech Solution

Penang's Commercial Kitchen Engineering Firm Since 2009

Founded in 2009, JR-Tech Solution Sdn Bhd is Penang's leading commercial kitchen engineering firm. We serve multinational corporations, premier hospitals, and major F&B chains — delivering end-to-end kitchen solutions from initial design through installation, maintenance, and chemical supply.

Our integrated approach means clients work with one expert partner from concept to commissioning and beyond.

Our Services
