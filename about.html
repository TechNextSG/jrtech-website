About JR-Tech Solution

16+ years engineering Malaysia's most demanding commercial kitchens.

Est.
2009
Founded in Penang, Malaysia

JR-Tech Solution Sdn Bhd was founded in Penang in 2009 with one goal: to become Malaysia's most trusted commercial kitchen engineering partner. Over 16 years we have grown from a small team into a full-service provider serving multinational corporations, premier hospitals, major F&B chains, and independent restaurateurs across the country.

Our strength is integration — we design, fabricate, supply, install, and maintain. Clients never need to look elsewhere. One partner, from concept to commissioning and beyond.
